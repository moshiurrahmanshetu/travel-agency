Day-By-Day Chronology

Curated Master Itinerary

Buyout Properties Only
Days 1 – 4 Aman Tokyo & Four Seasons Kyoto

Tokyo & Kyoto, Japan • The Imperial Awakening

Arrive at Tokyo Haneda via VIP tarmac transfer and check into Aman Tokyo suites. Enjoy an exclusive after-hours private viewing of the Mori Art Museum followed by a private jet hop to Kyoto.

Sovereign Highlight:

Private Zen meditation ceremony conducted by head monks at Tenryu-ji Temple, followed by an exclusive Geisha cultural soirée and multi-course Kaiseki dinner.

Days 5 – 8 Oberoi Amarvilas & Rambagh Palace

Agra & Jaipur, India • Mughal Palaces & Pink City

Touch down in Agra for direct private photography access inside the Taj Mahal gates at both sunrise and moonlight. Proceed to Jaipur residing in former royal residences.

Sovereign Highlight:

Private champagne elephant sanctuary blessing and VIP spectator seating at an exhibition polo match hosted by members of the Rajasthani royal family.

Days 9 – 13 Singita Grumeti Reserves

Serengeti Plains, Tanzania • The Wilderness Canvas

Transit directly to private airstrips inside the Grumeti reserve via executive Bush Hawk turbo-props. Reside in open-air luxury tented pavilions overlooking active watering holes.

Sovereign Highlight:

Dawn hot air ballooning across migrating wildebeest herds, followed by a champagne bush breakfast prepared by Singita’s executive private chefs.

Days 14 – 18 Le Sirenuse & Rosewood Castiglion del Bosco

Amalfi Coast & Tuscany, Italy • Mediterranean Renaissance

Arrive in Naples for private Riva yacht cruising along Positano's dramatic cliffs. Helicopter inland to the rolling hills of Val d'Orcia for private historic estate buyouts.

Sovereign Highlight:

Private truffle hunting expedition with master hounds, followed by an exclusive vintage Brunello di Montalcino cellar tasting hosted by the Marchesi Antinori family.

Days 19 – 24 Ritz Paris Imperial Suites

Easter Island & Paris Finale • The Imperial Soirée

Inspect the monolithic Moai sanctuaries of Rapa Nui with resident UNESCO directors. Conclude this transformative global voyage in Paris residing in Place Vendôme buyouts.

Sovereign Finale Gala:

Private after-hours buyout of the Palace of Versailles. Enjoy a white-glove farewell gala dinner inside the Hall of Mirrors accompanied by a chamber orchestra.
